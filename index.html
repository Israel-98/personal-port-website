Where We are Now

Celebrating 100 years of Bauhaus, I drew my inspiration from Paul Klee and Wassily Kandinsky. It’s my modern take on what Bauhaus art is today. The new Iphone 11 that was just released that year (2019), was my focal point of the design. By outlining the phone, still keeping the apple logo filled, and layering only a few centimeters next to the Iphone, that was my way of reminiscing on the past but remembering to stay in the present.

Dragon Brew

This Logo is inspired by a coffee shop, Dragon Brew. I wanted the logo to be simple but intriguing to look at. The light gradient gives the illusion the steam from the coffee cup is forming into a dragon.

Country Doctors Logo

This logo is inspired by the name, Country Doctors. I wanted to convey the idea the country life as well as the medical side, therefore I incorporated mountains, a doctor and it’s carriage. I wanted to stay away from just using black and white throughout the design so I used a gradient of blues and different hues of born to tie the design together as a whole, the colder colors gives it a relaxing feel as an overall piece.

FanEvent

I came up with the idea of FanEvent in Winter of 2020. FanEvent is a ticket selling app that connects to your Spotify and curates events based on your location and Spotify favorites. My recurring problem was that I always found out about concerts too late, with FanEvent, that problem was eliminated. You’re also allowed to make profiles and see what events your friends are attending.
